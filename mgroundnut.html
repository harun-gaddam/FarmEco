Basic Machinary

Plough

A plough is a tool or farm implement used in farming for initial cultivation of soil in preparation for sowing seed or planting to loosen or turn the soil.

Hand Cultivator

A hand cultivator is a gardening tool that is used to turn the soil where you plan on planting and for removing weeds.

Curved Knife Harrow

In agriculture, a harrow is an implement for breaking up and smoothing out the surface of the soil.harrowing may also be used to remove weeds and to cover seed after sowing.

Shovel

A shovel is a tool for digging, lifting, and moving bulk materials, such as soil, coal, gravel, snow, sand, or ore. Most shovels are hand tools consisting of a broad blade fixed to a medium-length handle.

Tractor

The farm tractor is used for pulling or pushing agricultural machinery or trailers, for plowing, tilling, disking, harrowing, planting, and similar tasks.
